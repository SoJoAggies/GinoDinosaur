Henry is a five-year-old boy who needs to go to the hospital for an operation. During the surgery (under anesthesia), Henry enters an imaginary world where he meets Seymour, a lovable long-necked green dinosaur. They embark on a series of adventures where Seymour helps Henry learn how to be brave.

An Adventure with Seymour the Dinosaur At the Hospital was inspired by the authorâ€™s own personal experience of undergoing surgeries as a child. The purpose of this book is to provide understanding and comfort for both parents and their children as they navigate this experience.
